Step into “Santa's Dessert Party,” where Christmas meets a sweet wonderland! The centerpiece is a giant layered cake topped with a Christmas tree, surrounded by oversized candies and colorful gift boxes. People can take photos on the giant melting ice-cream chair, children can enjoy the donut slide, and everyone can try the interactive ring toss game. The design blends festive cheer with a dessert-inspired twist, inviting all into a magical, sugary celebration.
