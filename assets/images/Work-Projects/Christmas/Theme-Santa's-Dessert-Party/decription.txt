For this theme, the main structure is a giant Christmas cake, designed as the centerpiece with a Christmas tree on top to create a strong festive focus. Around it, I added different dessert-inspired elements to make the whole space feel playful and immersive.

On the left side, there is a giant chair with melting ice cream, giving a fun and slightly whimsical look, almost like a fantasy dessert world. On the right side, there is also a small playground made with a donut structure, where it acts as a slide for interaction.

In front, I included an interactive ring toss game, where people can play by throwing rings, bringing in a funfair-style activity that fits the “party” concept.
